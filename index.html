Projects

DUT19

Together with a team of about 80 people we built a very quick and good looking electric formula, competing with it at three Formula Student events. Formula Student competitions are very large events where numerous teams present the cars that they have worked for an entire year. The car is tested in a series of static and dynamic events. The car's acceleration, efficiency, durability, overall design, production cost are all important aspects that are considered during the events. Our car can accelerate to 100 km/h in about 2.5 seconds! My task as a software engineer was mostly to maintain and test the code that ran on the main Electronic Control Unit.
